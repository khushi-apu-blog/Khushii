Thinking About the DIKW Pyramid

Data Information Knowledge Wisdom (DIKW) Model

Before this course, I had’nt learned or heard about the Data Information Knowledge Wisdom (DIKW) pyramid. So when I first saw it in class, I was thinking that we will learn about the data organizing. I thought it was just a simple way to explain how data will become useful. Then when i saw it in class , The diagram pyramid made it look very clear. First we have data, then it becomes information, then knowledge, and finally wisdom. And at that time, it felt like I’m getting a clear idea.

But after more class taken, I started to feel that it is not as simple as i thought. I get to know that the pyramid might be a good idea for a large amount of data. In starting i didn’t get what’s it actually for . Then in class some group activity took place. And it’s get more clear to me.Initially i have to read that again and again for understanding but later i get some sense of it.

For better understanding, in class our instructors have used many examples such as fitness tracker in mobile app , smart watch .Fitness tracker records daily steps and heart rate. All of these is data. Then this app also show graphs and weekly summaries, which is information.  But even after this information I don’t fully understand what is good for my health and what it’s not so from this I get to know that only information is not sufficient , knowledge is also important factor. It depends on individual that how he/she understand and use that information.  It is very much possible that different people can see the same data but understand it in different ways.

Overall, I understand that the DIKW pyramid is helpful for basic understanding of data, but it does not fully explain how complicated real life information or data actually is
